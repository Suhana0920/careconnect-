How It Works

When you log in, CareConnect+ checks your role and automatically redirects you to the appropriate dashboard:

  • 🔹 Patients → Patient Dashboard with appointment booking & medical records
  • 🔹 Doctors → Doctor Dashboard with patient appointments & prescription management
  • 🔹 Admins → Admin Dashboard with full system access & user management

Each role has different permissions, and users can only access features they're authorized for. This ensures data security and proper workflow management.
